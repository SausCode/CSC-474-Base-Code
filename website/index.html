Forest Fire

John Cunningham and Spencer Schurk - CSC 474 - Spring 2018 - Christian Eckhardt

Project Description

Forest Fire is a game about putting out fires in the forest. You are equipped with a state of the art firehose/jetpack cannon that gives you the power you need to put out all the fires. There is a small caveat to being gifted such a unique weapon, and that is you will need to refill the cannon when your water bar reaches 0% to continue fighting all of those fires.

In this project we were tasked with creating a unique and enjoyable graphics program that implemented multiple forms of animation. We chose a game as they are fun to play, and to make. Below you will find the different types of animation that are found in the game.

Animation Techniques Implemented:

  • Keyframe Animations
  • Dynamic Camera
  • Interactive Responding Animation
  • Particle Effects
  • Physical Animation
